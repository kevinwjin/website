Kevin Jin is a second-year PhD student in the Interdepartmental Program in Computational Biology and Biomedical Informatics at Yale University. He is advised by Professor Hua Xu in the Clinical NLP Lab, a research group in the Department of Biomedical Informatics and Data Science at Yale School of Medicine. He completed his undergraduate work at Johns Hopkins University, receiving a B.S. in Molecular and Cellular Biology in 2020.
